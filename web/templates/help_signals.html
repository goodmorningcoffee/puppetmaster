🔴 SMOKING_GUN signals (one match = confirmed)

⚪ WEAK / NOISE (filtered out)

CDNs (Cloudflare, AWS, Akamai, Fastly), common libraries (jQuery, Bootstrap), cloud provider abuse contacts, generic email prefixes (admin@, info@, ...), privacy-protected WHOIS markers, and ~150 other noise patterns are explicitly excluded.

The flood filter additionally suppresses any signal value shared by more than 50 domains as likely noise (configurable).
